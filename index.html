OVERVIEW

Gateways is the national level inter-collegiate post graduate IT fest organized by the Department of Computer Science, Christ University. It provides the much needed exposure which is a prerequisite to survive in the IT industry. Participants compete in numerous technical and non-technical events that are both entertaining and intellectually challenging.

We are proud to present the 21st version of Gateways, with which we continue our tradition to benefit the students in staying abreast with latest trends in technology and developing their organisational and soft skills.

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

  • Maximum of 15 participants per college.
  • Participants are required to carry their college ID cards.
  • All equipments like laptops, pen drives and cameras required for their events have to be carried by the participants.
  • Registration fee for each participant is Rs. 100, which is inclusive of all events and is non-refundable.
  • Registrations can be done on the spot or online.
  • Online registrations should be done before the 4th of September 2017.
  • Colleges registering online will get a 10% discount.
  • Outstation students should inform the fest organizers regarding their accommodation before the 4th of September 2017.
  • Participants must report at the venue 15 mins prior to the start of the event.
