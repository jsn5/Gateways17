Overview

ITC Infotech presents the third edition of iTech - the company’s flagship annual co-creation and technology innovation platform.

As part of iTech 2017, ITC Infotech is bringing together some of the sharpest technology minds to compete, create and code for complex business applications, as part of a programing Codeathon. Grab this opportunity to prove your mettle in technology and win the ultimate bragging rights, along with cash prizes and hiring opportunities, while you learn from industry experts.

An exciting addition to iTech 2017 for technology startups is the ITC Infotech Grant of Rs. 5 Lacs for a paid POC, and the opportunity to partner with the company, as we work towards creating a thriving ecosystem to accelerate technology innovation.

WHY PARTICIPATE
Students

If you are done with the text books and labs, come join us by sending in your entry for our programing codeathon. Our mentors and technology experts will guide you along the way to help you transform your idea. As a shortlisted candidate for ITC Infotech’s iTech 2017, you get the ultimate nationwide bragging rights, an opportunity to strengthen your CV and explore exciting career opportunities.

Developers & Professionals

Send in your entry now, and compete against some of the brightest technology minds. See how your idea measures up, interact with peers, and for 30 hours, be part of a race against time to create code for complex business applications. What’s more, you also get to interact with and learn from some pioneering start-ups as they showcase solutions they have developed and path-breaking innovations they are taking to market.

Startups

Join us for iTech and showcase your startup to business leaders from some of India’s biggest and most respected companies, as well as ITC Infotech’s global clients - all under the same roof. Here’s more – in addition to the ITC Infotech Grant of Rs. 5 Lacs for a paid-POC to the winning startup, as a system integrator, ITC Infotech may also partner and invest in helping you develop your solution, create a joint go-to-market and provide you with market access.

CODEATHON

Are you up for the challenge?

If you love programming, this is the opportunity to code your way to glory. Participation in the iTech Codeathon is free and open to all (students,Professionals and developers). This is a team based competition & each team can have a maximum of 4 members.

Participants need to select one of the Codeathon programing challenges listed in Themes Section and submit solution overview details. Entries will be judged on the overall technical approach, reusability, security and performance.

Codeathon Timeline

Idea Phase

Coding & Mentoring Phase


Each shortlisted team will be assigned a technical mentor.Teams are expected to develop & deploy their code on the ITC Infotech environment for verification.

Presentation & Evaluation phase


Finalists will be invited to Bengaluru to further enhance their code and present to an eminent jury.Top 3 teams will be fecilitated during the award ceremony.

CODEATHON
  • 1. How should I register for the challenge?
    Register as a new user, by clicking the "Register" button in the portal. Registration is mandatory for participating in the program.
  • 2. What is the eligibility for participation in Codeathon?
    Codeathon is open for Students,Developers & Professionals.
  • 3. What should be the team size?
    Each team can have a minimum of 2 & maximum of 4 members.
  • 4. Can there be individual participation?
    No, you can't register as an individual.
  • 5. Can we submit our solutions for more than one theme?
    No, you cannot submit solutions for more than one theme
  • 6. Can we submit multiple solutions for one theme?
    No. You are allowed to submit only one idea per theme.
  • 7. What are the different stages, tracks, challenges & timeline of this challenge?
    Please click here to know the details.
  • 8. Who will own the IP generated as part of the Codeathon?
    Code submitted to ITC Infotech during iTech 2017 will become the property of ITC Infotech, and the company reserves the right to use the code in full or in part or modify the code
  • 9. When and where the winners be declared?
    Winners will be announced at the end of the codeathon event, which will be held on Sunday, 23rd July, 2017 at ITC Infotech campus in Bengaluru.Click here to know more about the agenda.
  • 10. Where will the final codeathon event be conducted?
    The final codeathon event will be held at ITC Infotech Campus at Bengaluru on 22nd & 23rd July, 2017.Click here to know the agenda.

    No
